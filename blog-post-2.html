Top 5 Backlink Strategies That Still Work

Backlinks still matter, but only when they are relevant, trusted and naturally placed. Here are five strategies Rankvia recommends for safe SEO growth.

01. Digital PR & News Mentions

Brand mentions and links from trusted publications help strengthen authority, credibility and search trust signals.

02. Curated Niche Edits

Niche edits place your backlink inside already indexed and relevant articles, making the placement look natural and useful.

03. Topical Authority Links

Relevant backlinks from websites in your industry are more valuable than random links from unrelated websites.

04. Guest Posting On Quality Sites

Guest posts still work when the website has quality content, real audience, good authority and niche relevance.

05. Content-Led Link Building

Helpful guides, case studies and research-style content attract better links and support long-term organic growth.
